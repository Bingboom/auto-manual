      \section{IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION}

   .. raw:: latex

      \safetywarning{INSTRUCTIONS PERTAINING TO RISK OF FIRE, ELECTRIC SHOCK, OR INJURY TO PERSONS}

   .. raw:: latex

      \begin{safetytwocol}


   .. raw:: latex

      \safetylead{Always follow these basic precautions when using Jackery Battery 1000.}


      - Read all the instructions before using Jackery Battery 1000.
   - Do not allow children to play on Jackery Battery 1000. Close supervision of children is necessary when Jackery Battery 1000 is used near children.
   - Stop using Jackery Battery 1000 immediately if it has been physically damaged or modified. Improper use may cause unpredictable behavior, leading to fire, explosion, or injury.



   .. raw:: latex

      \end{safetytwocol}

   .. raw:: latex

      \safetysubbar{OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS}

   .. raw:: latex

      \begin{safetytwocol}


   .. raw:: latex

      \safetylead{SAVE THESE INSTRUCTIONS}


      - Do not charge Jackery Battery 1000 in extremely hot or cold environments; follow the specified temperature ranges.
     - Charging temperature: -20°C to 45°C
     - Discharging temperature: -20°C to 45°C
   - Do not expose Jackery Battery 1000 to fire or excessive temperatures. Doing so may result in an explosion if the temperature exceeds the rated limit.



   .. raw:: latex

      \end{safetytwocol}
